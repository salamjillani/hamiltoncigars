Tasting Guide

Enhance your cigar experience with proper tasting techniques

1

Preparation

Allow your cigar to rest at room temperature for 30 minutes. Use a quality cutter for a clean cut.

2

Lighting

Use a butane lighter or cedar spill. Toast the foot evenly before taking your first draw.

3

Tasting

Take slow, deliberate draws. Let the smoke rest on your palate to identify flavor notes.

4

Progression

Note how flavors evolve throughout the smoke. Each third offers unique characteristics.
