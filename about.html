Our Legacy

A tradition of excellence spanning generations

Rooted in Heritage: The Dominican Legacy of Cigar Excellence

The story of Hamilton Cigars begins where earth, time, and tradition converge—on the rich, sun-drenched soil of the Dominican Republic. Revered as the soul of the modern cigar world, the Dominican Republic is home to centuries-old tobacco-growing traditions passed down through generations of artisans.

Following the Cuban Revolution, many of the world's most gifted cigar makers journeyed to the Dominican Republic, bringing with them their knowledge, seeds, and passion. What they found was a land with the perfect climate, fertile valleys, and patient craftsmanship needed to birth cigars of unmatched complexity and character.

At Hamilton Cigars, we honor this lineage. Every leaf we select is grown, aged, and hand-rolled with intention—drawing from the legacy of master blenders while embracing the standards of modern luxury. Our cigars are more than indulgence; they are a living tribute to the Dominican Republic's iconic role in the global cigar renaissance.

The Hamilton Heritage

Founded in 1847, Hamilton Cigars represents over 175 years of uncompromising dedication to the art of premium cigar craftsmanship. What began as a small family operation in the heart of tobacco country has evolved into a symbol of luxury and excellence recognized by connoisseurs worldwide.

Our founder, Master Tobacconist James Hamilton, established the core principles that guide us today: select only the finest tobacco leaves, employ traditional hand-rolling techniques, and never compromise on quality for quantity. These values have been passed down through five generations of the Hamilton family.

A Founder's Note: From Brooklyn to the Barrel Room

Hamilton Hypolite, a proud New Yorker and Haitian by heritage, carries more than a name—he carries a legacy. Raised in the rhythm of Brooklyn and now rooted in Atlanta, Hamilton is a father of two daughters, a visionary, and a connoisseur of culture. His journey is one of balance: honoring tradition while building new rituals.

Hamilton Cigars was born from a desire to elevate the cigar experience—bridging generations, celebrating craftsmanship, and offering a moment of pause in an ever-moving world.

"This brand is my love letter to culture, to legacy, and to every man or woman finding peace in their own rhythm."

"As a father of two daughters, everything I build has to stand the test of time. Hamilton Cigars is more than a product—it's a standard. A mark of excellence my daughters can one day be proud of."

Whether you're toasting to milestones or simply reclaiming a moment for yourself, Hamilton Cigars invites you to savor each draw with intention, style, and soul.

This isn't just a cigar. This is a lifestyle.

Artisan Craftsmanship

Every Hamilton cigar is hand-rolled by master artisans who have dedicated their lives to perfecting their craft. Our tobacco is sourced from the world's premier growing regions, aged in cedar-lined rooms for a minimum of three years, and carefully selected for consistency and flavor profile.

From the fertile valleys of Nicaragua to the sun-drenched fields of the Dominican Republic, we work exclusively with generational tobacco farmers who share our commitment to excellence. Each leaf is inspected, sorted, and aged using methods unchanged since our founding.
