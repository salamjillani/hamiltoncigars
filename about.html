Our Legacy

A tradition of excellence spanning generations

The Hamilton Heritage

Founded in 1847, Hamilton Cigars represents over 175 years of uncompromising dedication to the art of premium cigar craftsmanship. What began as a small family operation in the heart of tobacco country has evolved into a symbol of luxury and excellence recognized by connoisseurs worldwide.

Our founder, Master Tobacconist James Hamilton, established the core principles that guide us today: select only the finest tobacco leaves, employ traditional hand-rolling techniques, and never compromise on quality for quantity. These values have been passed down through five generations of the Hamilton family.

Artisan Craftsmanship

Every Hamilton cigar is hand-rolled by master artisans who have dedicated their lives to perfecting their craft. Our tobacco is sourced from the world's premier growing regions, aged in cedar-lined rooms for a minimum of three years, and carefully selected for consistency and flavor profile.

From the fertile valleys of Nicaragua to the sun-drenched fields of the Dominican Republic, we work exclusively with generational tobacco farmers who share our commitment to excellence. Each leaf is inspected, sorted, and aged using methods unchanged since our founding.

Our Values

Uncompromising Quality

Every cigar meets our exacting standards before earning the Hamilton name. We reject more cigars than we sell, ensuring only perfection reaches our customers.

Traditional Methods

Hand-rolling techniques passed down through generations combined with modern aging processes create cigars of exceptional complexity and character.

Sustainable Excellence

We partner with farmers who practice sustainable agriculture, ensuring the finest tobacco while preserving the land for future generations.
